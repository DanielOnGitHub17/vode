Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Vode

How does Vode work?

Vode provides a unified platform for scheduling technical interviews, conducting live coding sessions, scoring candidates objectively, and collaborating with your team. Simply invite candidates, they join your virtual interview room, and you evaluate their technical skills in real-time with our built-in code editor. The platform handles everything from scheduling logistics to performance analytics, making the entire interview process seamless and efficient.

Is my candidate data secure?

Absolutely. Vode uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption, is SOC 2 Type II compliant, and GDPR ready. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your candidate data with third parties without explicit consent. Your data is hosted on secure, redundant servers with automatic backups and disaster recovery protocols in place.

Can I integrate Vode with my ATS?

Yes! Vode integrates seamlessly with popular ATS platforms like Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and 50+ other tools via API and Zapier. We can also build custom integrations for enterprise clients. Candidates can be synced automatically, interview results flow back to your ATS instantly, and all data remains synchronized in real-time.

What programming languages do you support?

Vode supports 25+ programming languages including JavaScript, Python, Java, C++, Go, Rust, Ruby, PHP, TypeScript, C#, Kotlin, and more. You can customize the available languages for your team and create language-specific coding challenges. Our code environment includes syntax highlighting, auto-completion, and real-time compilation feedback.

How much does Vode cost?

Vode offers flexible pricing with options for teams of all sizes. Start with our Free tier for testing and evaluation, then scale to Pro ($499/month) or Enterprise plans as your hiring volume grows. Most teams see ROI within 2-3 months through reduced hiring costs and improved candidate quality. Custom pricing is available for enterprises with unique requirements.
